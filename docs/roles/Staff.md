# Staff Role

The **Staff** role includes all non-teaching personnel responsible for HR and administrative logistics.

## Primary Responsibilities
- **HR Support**: Assisting in maintaining staff master records and documents.
- **Operations**: Managing office supplies, facility logistics, and administrative tasks.
- **Support**: Assisting in the execution of school events and communication distribution.
- **Leave Management**: Submitting and tracking their own leave applications in [[Leave Management]].

## Access Level
- **Limited Access**: Focused primarily on their own professional records.
- **No Access**: To sensitive academic data (marks) or financial records (unless authorized for HR).
