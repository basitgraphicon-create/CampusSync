# Principal Role

The **Principal** overseen the academic integrity and overall performance of the institution.

## Primary Responsibilities
- **Academic Supervision**: Reviewing lesson plans, teacher attendance, and syllabus completion.
- **Reporting**: Analyzing student performance trends across all classes.
- **Approvals**: Finalizing examination results and report cards.
- **Policy Enforcement**: Ensuring adherence to the school's academic calendar and rules.
- **Staff Evaluation**: Monitoring teacher effectiveness and professional development.

## Access Level
- **Broad Read Access**: Full visibility across all academic modules.
- **Approval Rights**: Authorized to finalize and lock exam results.
- **Restricted Write**: Limited to high-level academic oversight.
