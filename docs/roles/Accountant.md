# Accountant Role

The **Accountant** is responsible for the financial discipline and health of the school.

## Primary Responsibilities
- **Fee Collection**: Recording payments and issuing receipts in [[Receipt and Collection]].
- **Ledger Management**: Updating student accounts and tracking outstanding balances via [[Student Fee Application]].
- **Financial Reporting**: Generating daily collections, income/expense reports, and cashbooks in [[Financial Reporting]].
- **Payroll**: Processing staff salaries and advances in [[Payroll Management]].
- **Fine Management**: Applying and waiving late fines based on [[Fee Structure Engine|School Policy]].

## Access Level
- **Full Control**: Over the financial and payroll modules.
- **View-only**: For student and staff master records.
- **No Access**: To exam marks or academic structure configurations.
