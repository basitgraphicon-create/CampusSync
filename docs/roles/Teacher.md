# Teacher Role

The **Teacher** role is the primary interface for academic data and student interaction.

## Primary Responsibilities
- **Attendance**: Daily marking of student presence via [[Attendance Management]].
- **Academics**: Managing homework, class notes, and syllabus progress via the [[Communication Module]].
- **Examination**: Entering student scores in the [[Marks Entry]] module.
- **Communication**: Sending class-wide messages or individual updates via the [[Communication Module]].
- **Class Management**: Monitoring student behavior and academic growth within their assigned sections.

## Access Level
- **Functional Access**: Restricted to modules relevant to their assigned classes and subjects.
- **Read/Write**: For attendance and marks entry.
- **View-only**: For student master records (restricted fields).
