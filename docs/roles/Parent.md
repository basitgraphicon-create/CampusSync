# Parent Role

The **Parent** (or Guardian) role is designed for transparency and engagement.

## Primary Responsibilities
- **Monitoring**: Viewing their child's attendance and academic progress.
- **Financial Compliance**: Checking due fees and historical payment receipts.
- **Communication**: Receiving official circulars, announcements, and teacher updates.
- **Personal Information**: Viewing but not editing the [[Student Master Record]].
- **Results**: Accessing their child's report cards once published by the [[Principal]].

## Access Level
- **Read-only**: Restricted entirely to data concerning their own children.
- **No Access**: To internal school configurations, payroll, or other students' data.
