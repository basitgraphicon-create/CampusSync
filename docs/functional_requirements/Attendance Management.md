# Attendance Management

## Overview
The Attendance module tracks the daily presence and absence of students and staff, which is critical for academic eligibility and payroll.

## Key Features
- **Daily Class Attendance**: Simple interface for class teachers to mark student attendance for the entire class.
- **Period-wise Attendance**: Granular tracking for secondary levels where students may attend different subjects/periods.
- **Teacher Attendance**: System for marking and tracking the attendance of teaching and non-teaching staff.
- **Leave Marking**: Integration with leave management to automatically mark students/staff as 'On Leave'.
- **Monthly Percentage Calculation**: Automated calculation of attendance percentages for reporting.
- **Printable Attendance Reports**: Generate monthly or term-wise attendance sheets for student records.

## Implementation Details (To be expanded)
- Integration with SMS/Email module for automated absence notification to parents.
- Audit history of attendance modifications.
- Handling of holidays and weekends in calculations.
