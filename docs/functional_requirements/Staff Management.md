# Staff Management

## Overview
The Staff Management module is the central repository for all employee information, ensuring organized HR operations.

## Key Features
- **Staff Master Records**: Comprehensive profiles for every employee, including personal, contact, and emergency details.
- **Designation and Department Mapping**: Organizational hierarchy management to track roles (e.g., Teacher, Admin, Staff) and departments (e.g., Science, Admin).
- **Qualification Details**: Record educational background, certifications, and specialized skills.
- **Document Storage**: Secure digital storage for IDs, contracts, and qualification certificates.
- **Joining and Exit Tracking**: Formal records of hiring dates, probationary periods, and resignation/termination details.

## Implementation Details (To be expanded)
