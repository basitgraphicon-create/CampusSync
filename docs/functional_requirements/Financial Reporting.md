# Financial Reporting

## Overview
The Financial Reporting module provides administrators with a comprehensive view of the school's financial health and student-wise balances.

## Key Features
- **Student Ledger Statements**: Individualised reports for parents and students showing their complete financial history with the school.
- **Daily Collection Report**: Summary of all daily transactions for administrative oversight.
- **Outstanding Report**: List of all students with unpaid fees, filterable by class, fee group, or due date.
- **Cashbook**: A transaction-driven record of all cash inflows and outflows.
- **Income/Expense Summary**: High-level overview of the school's profitability and budget adherence.

## Implementation Details (To be expanded)
- Export capabilities (PDF, Excel) for all reports.
- Role-based visibility for sensitive financial data.
- Ledger-based accounting principles for consistent financial integrity.
