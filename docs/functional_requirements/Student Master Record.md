# Student Master Record

## Overview
The Student Master Record is the central repository for all information related to an individual student. It serves as the single source of truth for administrative and academic purposes.

## Data Components
- **Personal Details**: Name, Date of Birth, Gender, Blood Group, Aadhaar Number, etc.
- **Guardian Details**: Support for multiple guardians (Father, Mother, Local Guardian) with contact information and occupation.
- **Contact Information**: Current and permanent addresses, primary and secondary mobile numbers, and email addresses.
- **Category & Demographics**: Caste, Religion, Mother Tongue, and other demographic markers for regulatory reporting.
- **Document Upload Support**: Digital storage for Birth Certificates, Caste Certificates, previous School Leaving Certificates, etc.
- **Sibling Linking**: Ability to link records of siblings within the same school for family-wise fee concessions or communication.
- **Status Tracking**: Current status (Active, Inactive, Suspended, Alumni) and history of status changes.
