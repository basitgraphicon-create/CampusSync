# Payroll Management

## Overview
The Payroll module automates the calculation and disbursement of staff salaries, ensuring accuracy and timely payments.

## Key Features
- **Define Salary Structure**: Configure base pay, allowances (e.g., HRA, DA), and deductions (e.g., PF, Tax).
- **Generate Monthly Payroll**: Automated batch processing to calculate net pay for all employees.
- **Generate Payslips**: Professional payslips for each employee, showing a detailed breakdown of earnings and deductions.
- **Track Salary Payments**: Record the date and method of payment for every staff member.
- **Manage Salary Advances**: Handle loans or advances given to employees, with automated deduction from subsequent payrolls.

## Implementation Details (To be expanded)
- Integration with the Attendance module for 'Loss of Pay' (LOP) calculations.
- Support for statutory compliance reports (e.g., Provident Fund contributions).
- Role-based oversight for payroll approval and processing.
